The Snuts

Who are The Snuts?

The Snuts are a four-piece indie-rock band from Whitburn, Scotland. The band consists of Jack Cochrane on vocals/guitar, Joe McGillveray on guitar, Callum Wilson on bass and Jordan McKay on drums.Friends since school, the band have been making music together since the age of 15.

Their Mixtape EP smashed into the charts – reaching number one in the Scotland and Vinyl Album Charts and number 14 in the UK official list. Songs like fan Fatboy Slim, All Your Friends and Coffee and Cigarettes, capture The Snuts at their scintillating, anthemic best – providing a snapshot of what we can look forward to from their first full length.

Drawing comparisons with early Arctic Monkeys, Kings of Leon and Oasis, West Lothian quartet THE SNUTS emerged as a word-of-mouth phenomenon throughout Scotland.

The Snuts’ ascent shows no sign of slowing down with major support from the likes of BBC 6 Music, Radio 1 and Clash magazine. New single Elephants took the charts by storm in March 2020 going straight in at number 1 on the iTunes chart.
