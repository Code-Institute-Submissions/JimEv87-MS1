Who are The Snuts?

The Snuts are a four-piece indie-rock band from Whitburn, Scotland. The band consists of Jack Cochrane on vocals/guitar, Joe McGillveray on guitar, Callum Wilson on bass and Jordan McKay on drums.

Friends since school, the band have been making music together since the age of 15.

Drawing comparisons with early Arctic Monkeys, Kings of Leon and Oasis, West Lothian quartet THE SNUTS emerged as a word-of-mouth phenomenon throughout Scotland.
